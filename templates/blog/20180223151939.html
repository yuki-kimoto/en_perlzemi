Modulo operator "%" - find the remainder

You can use the modulo operator "%" to find the remainder of numbers. 
my $num3 = $num1 %$num2;
This is an example to find the remainder of 11 divided by 3. 
my $num = 11% 3;
The remainder will be 2.
