pop function-extracts the last element of an array

Use the pop function to retrieve the last element of the array . 
$last = pop @array;

Sample

This is a sample of the pop function. If @nums is (1, 2, 3), 3 is assigned to $last and @nums becomes (1, 2). 
my @nums = (1, 2, 3);
my $last = pop @nums;
